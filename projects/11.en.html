Nebul√¶ is a virtual reality experience, dedicated to dance, in which a universe of particles reacts to the user's gestures. It is possible to experiment modified non realistic physics and to express them on stars dust in order to propose a new experience to the dancer through his or her gestures.

This project is inspired by the PIXEL ballet, direction by the choreographer Mourad Merzouki and digital art by Adrien Mondot and Claire Bardainne, allowing a user to interact in real-time with particles.

Shifting from real world physic to virtual ones allow for a unique aesthetic experience. At the same time, the dancer visualizes the environment in a HTC Vive headset and the spectators can watch it in the Cave.
